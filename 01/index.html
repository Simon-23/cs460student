RO.ME "3 DREAMS OF BLACK"

RO.ME is a interactive musical film by Chris Milk based on the song called 3 Dreams of black by Danger Mouse, Daniele Luppi & Norah Jones. RO.ME was created in 2011 using WebGL where users were able to steer themselves throughout the narrative. This was huge at that time since WebGL and html 5 enabled devlopers to create realistic interactive 2D & 3D graphical models on the web by using your computers video card which was never possible before. By creating something like RO.ME, it shows that you can have more than just standard images and videos on the web. It opens up the opportunity and possibilies to have real time dynamic graphics rendering while you experience the film.

Technology: In this film, 2D and 3D graphics were implemented along with video, audio, and multimedia all combined together to create this film through WebGL. Majority of the film was created not using video but rather a real time rendering of the graphical models through WebGL, html 5, and javascript. WebGL is hardware accelerated which means it uses your videocard to render the graphical models really fast. This is what allows users to interact with the film in real time very smoothly.
